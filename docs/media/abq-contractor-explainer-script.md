# Script — ProBridge ABQ Contractor Explainer (≈45 seconds)

## Working Title
“ABQ pros: More jobs, less noise”

## Audience
Local contractors in ABQ: handymen, cleaners, movers, assembly and home repair pros.

## Goal
Explain why contractors should join ProBridge ABQ and accept jobs.

---

## Script

**0–5s — Hook**

VO:  
“ABQ pros — tired of chasing leads that never turn into real work?”

VISUAL:  
Contractor staring at a phone with unanswered messages and unpaid invoices.

---

**5–15s — Introduce ProBridge ABQ**

VO:  
“ProBridge ABQ sends you ready-to-go jobs with clear scope and payout, so you can spend more time working and less time chasing.”

VISUAL:  
Transition from chaotic messages to a clean job offer screen with payout shown.

---

**15–25s — How it works for contractors**

VO:  
“Tell us what you do and where in ABQ you work. When a job matches your skills and area, you see the details, see the payout, and choose whether to accept.”

VISUAL:  
Contractor portal:  
- Categories selected  
- Map radius  
- Job offer card with payout amount.

---

**25–35s — Money and control**

VO:  
“No monthly fees, no bidding wars. You only pay a platform fee when you get paid, and you decide which jobs to take.”

VISUAL:  
Payout breakdown animation: job total vs contractor payout, without exposing exact platform percent on-screen.

---

**35–42s — Payout timing and support**

VO:  
“Our goal in ABQ is fast payouts after job completion, with support if anything gets in the way.”

VISUAL:  
Calendar or clock icon with “Payout after job completion” highlight.

---

**42–45s — Call to action**

VO:  
“If you’re a reliable ABQ pro and want a better way to get work, apply to ProBridge ABQ today.”

ON-SCREEN TEXT:  
“Apply for ABQ jobs”  
Contractor signup URL.

---

## Notes for Production

- Show real contractor scenarios: tools, small crews, homes.
- Tone should be respectful and opportunity-focused, not hype.
- Keep on-screen text simple and legible.

# ABQ Video Briefs

This document summarizes each video deliverable for ProBridge ABQ.

---

## 1. Client Explainer — 60s

- **ID:** client_explainer_60s
- **Audience:** ABQ homeowners and renters
- **Objective:** Explain ProBridge and drive first-time job requests.
- **Script source:** `docs/media/abq-client-explainer-script.md`
- **Key beats:**
  - Problem: phone tag and confusion.
  - Solution: one request, one clear price, vetted local pro.
  - Flow: request → quote → approve & pay → job done.
  - CTA: “Request a local pro now.”

---

## 2. Contractor Explainer — 45s

- **ID:** contractor_explainer_45s
- **Audience:** ABQ contractors
- **Objective:** Drive signups and job acceptance.
- **Script source:** `docs/media/abq-contractor-explainer-script.md`
- **Key beats:**
  - Problem: bad leads, no-shows, wasted time.
  - Solution: jobs, not leads; clear payout; pros choose jobs.
  - Money: platform fee only when paid; fast payout target.
  - CTA: “Apply for ABQ jobs.”

---

## 3. Client Social Hooks — 15s

- **ID:** client_social_15s
